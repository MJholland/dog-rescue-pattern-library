About us

Loyal Rescue is a foster based rescue and we do not operate a shelter. We do not have viewing hours as each pet is in a private home. Should you be the successful applicant for a pet, you are welcome to come and meet the dog. You are not obligated to adopt, should you determine the fit is not correct, however we must go through this process prior to meeting the dog.

The Steps

  1. Your application will under go an initial review by a Loyal volunteer. Within a few days of sending your application a Loyal volunteer will be in touch to set up a phone interview. Phone interviews last approxiamtly 30 minutes.
  2. Your references will be contacted and the Loyal volunteer will wumbit a report to the Loyal Adoption Comitee. Once results are in, Loyal will request a home visit.
  3. Depending on location, the home visit will be completed days or weeks after the interview. Rescue work is largely volunteer based and will effect visiting availability.

Once these steps are complete and successful, you will be notified that you have been approved to adopt.
